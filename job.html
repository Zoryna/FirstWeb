Psychologist

The definition of a psychologist: A specialist in psychology who studies the brain, mind, and its mental states and processes.

Why be a Psychologist?

I want to be a cognitive psychologist because I find the human mind interesting. I want to learn more about human behavior through research.

There are different types of Psychologists

  • A Clinical Psychologist can diagnose patients through treatment, personality tests, and psychotherapy.
  • A Developmental Psychologist studies how people grew up to be how they are.
  • A School Psychologist assists students with their emotional needs and they help with the student's behavior at school.
